Course Administration

See in the course dependencies and the learning outcomes of your courses at first sight.
Which course do you have to visit in order to visit the course following up?

Individual Timetable

See your up-to-date timetable depending on your selected courses – not only on your PC but on your smartphone right in the moment you need the information.

ECTS overview

You don’t know, how many ECTS you reached so far? This is history!
Now you can easily see how many ECTS you have and how many you need for your grade.
All in clear diagrams in your personal area.

    All this questions are answered in DegreeOverview on your personal dashboard!
